We're looking forward to welcoming you in our completely renovated 19th century farm. Rest with us after a day of hiking or cycling.

More about the house >

Rooms and prices

You can both reserve for one night, or for multiple nights to discover the area. For any reservation, breakfast is included.

Price per night (incl. breakfast)

€30,- for 1 person.
€45,- for 2 persons.

The price includes bedding, towels and coffee/tea. On request lunch can be provided as well.

Please enquire about staying with more than 2 people.

The rooms

We have two 2-person rooms. One on the first floor, where you'll also find the bathroom, and one on the ground floor which features folding doors to the inner garden and terrace. When the weather allows we like to serve breakfast on the terrace.

Location

HuisOnderweg is located east of the center of Roermond. Just a short 10 minute walk from the train station (and the center of the city), and appoximately 25 minutes from the 'Pieterpad'. The LF-3 (national cycling route #3) passes the train station.

Important!

In the municipality of Roermond there are two streets called 'Roermondsestraat'. The other one is in the village Herten (about 3km. away) and you'll probably be directed to that if you ask for the 'Roermondsestraat'. HuisOnderweg is located at the Roermondsestraat which is a sidestreet of the 'Gebroek' (opposite of Gebroek 35). The best way to get here is to ask after the 'Gebroek'. Your navigation will point you in the right direction if you add our postal code: 6043 GV, and housenumber: 2.
