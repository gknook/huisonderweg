Directions to HuisOnderweg

You can either come to our home from the Pieterpad. Directions are indicated below, as well as the routes to take when coming from the train station or arriving by car.

Directions from the Pieterpad

When walking the Pieterpad route you're coming down the red trail indicated above. To get to our home you have to follow the blue path on the map. The steps to take our described below. It's about 25 minutes walking.

1. Coming down the Pieterpad route, you'll find a hiking route junction point (knooppunt) directing to junctionpoint 'knooppunt 80'. Follow the route to that junction point, it will follow the (creek/ponds) to the left.
2. When you arrive at junction point 80, go straight a little further in the direction of junction point 45, until you see a small chapel.
3. Go left at the chapel, into the Roermondsestraat.
4. In the Roermondsestraat our home is directly to the right. It's the only yellow house in the street, we're looking forward to welcoming you!

Directions from the train station

Walking or cycling

Standing with your back to the station the easiest way to get to our home is to walk left on the 'Willem II singel', at the first junction you go left again underneath the tunnel. Directly after the tunnel go right, onto the Maria Theresialaan. This street has a slight climb and turns left when you reach the top of the climb. Follow the street which is now called the 'Hendriklaan'. At the end of the Hendriklaan there's a T-crossing with the 'bredeweg'. The Roermondsestraat directly follows the Henrdiklaan across the Bredeweg, just 3 meters lower. To get there go left for 20 meters to find the footpath that leads down to the Roermondesestraat. Our home is at the end of it to the left.

Directions by car (A73)

Take exit 20 towards Roermond Oost, then follow direction 'Centrum/Ziekenhuis(H/Laurentius)'. After the first roundabout go straight (Koninginnelaan) until the second roundabout. Take a right there (onto the Bredeweg) and follow until the first opportunity to go to the right (approx. 700m, Nassaustraat). After another 50m. take a right, onto the Gebroek. Take that street till you find the Roermondsestraat to the right (opposite of Gebroek 35).
