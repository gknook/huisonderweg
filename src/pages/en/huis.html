Huisonderweg

We're looking forward to welcoming you in our home, especially after a day of hiking or cycling. You'll be looking forward to a good bed in a quiet environment. Our home is a completely renovated former farm located along the Roermondsestraat, which is the street that used to connect the little village 'het Gebroek' to the city of Roermond. Nowadays it's part of Roermond, and located a short 10 minute walk from the center. The little farm can be seen on records from before 1900, and some of the old structures are still visible. The last renovation happened in 2000, when we tried to harmonize the layout of the house amongst others by using lots of colour.
