Roermond

As a city Roermond has always been important to it's region of 'Midden Limburg'. As a tourist attraction the inner city has several historic landmarks and a vibrant shopping centre. Around Roermond you can attempt water sports in the area around the 'De Maasplassen', or enjoy the nature around us. East to Roermond, near the German border, you can enjoy a walk trough the forest and nature area 'De Meinweg'. If you decide to go west another area called 'Leudal' can be visited. Both areas have ample opportunities for cycling and hiking.

For a day-trip you might want to consider visiting the quaint little white village of Thorn, or going to the Belgian Maaseik.
